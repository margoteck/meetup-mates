3beards was a small London-based team of passionate people who supported the local and global startup community with regular, meaningful events.

Five years on the startup scene had grown and matured, and so the question whether all of 3beards activities were still relevant, had to be asked. The decision was made to activate a pivot of sorts and focus our energy on two of our main projects:
